Oslomet Logo

The logo of OsloMet is an iconic symbol representing the university's identity and values. The design is carefully crafted to convey a sense of innovation, knowledge, and inclusivity. The logo consists of carefully selected elements that reflect OsloMet's mission and vision. The color palette is harmonious and inviting, with colors symbolizing diversity, creativity, and a dynamic learning environment.

The new logo

The new OsloMet logo represents a fresh chapter in the university's visual identity. Meticulously crafted, it serves as a contemporary symbol, encapsulating the spirit of innovation, knowledge, and inclusivity that defines the institution. Through carefully selected elements, the design mirrors OsloMet's current mission and vision, reflecting a commitment to progress and relevance in a dynamic academic landscape. The harmonious and inviting color palette not only embraces diversity but also signifies a creative and vibrant learning environment. The new logo stands as a visual testament to OsloMet's forward-looking approach, embodying the values and aspirations that guide the university into the future.

Old Logo

The old logo of OsloMet carried with it a rich history and a sense of continuity. Serving as a visual embodiment of the university's heritage, the design encapsulated the essence of its journey through time. Each element within the logo held significance, reflecting the values and aspirations of OsloMet. While the design might have evolved over the years, the old logo remains a testament to the institution's roots and the milestones it has achieved. It stood as a symbol of the university's past, a reminder of the progress made, and a nod to the enduring spirit that continues to shape OsloMet's identity.
